Get to know Rick Brown

Business philosophy built on trust, education, and long-term client relationships

Rick Brown knows that the purchase or sale of real estate is one of the largest financial commitments most people will ever make. He is determined to support this experience in a successful, satisfying way for his clients. Compassionate and full of integrity, Rick works to establish not only a professional relationship but also a friendship with his clients.

Striving to make the real estate transaction successful and pleasant are the hallmarks of Rick's career. These are just a few of the reasons why he has become one of the most sought after real estate professionals in the area.

Educating is an important step Rick takes to help his clients make an informed decision and to ensure their complete satisfaction. He always shares all options and helps them reason through any difficult situation. This, combined with his patience, willing nature, in-depth industry knowledge and dedication, is why Rick maintains a client relationship beyond the closing of the transaction.

Communities served

Rick specializes in the sale and marketing of homes in Vernon Hills, Mundelein, Libertyville, Gurnee, Grayslake, Buffalo Grove, Green Oaks, Lindenhurst, Lake Villa, the Round Lake area, Lake County, Northern Cook County, and McHenry County.

Personal background

A native of Illinois, Rick has lived in the state for most of his life. Beginning in high school, Rick worked for 12 years in the grocery business advancing to a managerial position both in Illinois and Hawaii. Working in management within this very competitive and customer-service oriented field led to being hired by a North Suburban builder to manage their diverse portfolio of properties in the Chicagoland area.

Rick is a devoted father of two boys in their 20s. He has been very involved with his children's education and activities, volunteering at local schools, chaperoning field trips, and coaching basketball for the park district. In his spare time Rick enjoys the theater, snow skiing, photography, and movies.
